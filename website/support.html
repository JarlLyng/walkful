Help with Walkful for iPhone

Fastest way to reach us: support@iamjarl.com

Contact

Email support@iamjarl.com and you'll hear back from a human — usually within a couple of days. To help us help you quickly, please include:

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version (Settings → General → About).
  • The Walkful version (shown at the bottom of the app's Settings tab).
  • What you expected to happen, and what happened instead. A screenshot helps a lot.

Not seeing your steps?

Walkful reads your activity from Apple Health, so if everything shows zero, Health access is usually the reason. iOS doesn't tell apps when read access is declined, so Walkful can't detect this directly. To check:

  1. Open the iPhone Settings app.
  2. Go to Health → Data Access & Devices → Walkful.
  3. Turn on the categories you want Walkful to read (steps at minimum).

If access is on and steps still look wrong, open the Apple Health app and check that steps appear there — Walkful shows the same merged total that Health does.

Widget not updating?

The Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets refresh on a schedule that iOS controls, so a short delay after a walk is normal. Opening the app refreshes them immediately. If a widget seems stuck, removing it and adding it again usually resolves it.

Purchases and refunds

Walkful Pro is a one-time purchase — no subscription to cancel. If you've bought Pro before (or on another device with the same Apple Account), use Restore purchase on the Pro screen. Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us: visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple Account.

Privacy questions

Walkful collects nothing — no accounts, no servers, no tracking. The full details are in the privacy policy. Since your data never leaves your device, there is no account to delete and nothing for us to look up; deleting the app deletes everything.

Feature requests and bugs

Walkful is open source. If you're technically inclined, you can browse known issues or report bugs directly on GitHub — otherwise email works just as well.
